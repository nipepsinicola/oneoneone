WiWiWi Cat

About

Wi Wi Wi Cat or WiWiWi Cat refers to a video of an orange kitten eating food and making sounds that resemble "wi wi wi," "uyaya" and "wu wu wi," as well as another video of a different kitten sitting in its food bowl that uses the same audio
